Rhymes from Dhanbad

And The City of Nawaabs

Streets of Dhanbad & Lucknow span the poetic world that resides within the pages of Rhymes from Dhanbad - a two-part poetry collection spanning two cities. This collection of 39 poems also tells a tale of the author's life from a poetic viewpoint.

Rhymes from Dhanbad ventures into fantasy, philosophy, love, & heartbreak. From within its pages, it starts a journey that takes the reader from the confines of home down a hazy memory lane. Simultaneously, it showcases the cruel acts and immoral ethics of leaders and politicians that have failed humanity. Sometimes nostalgic, sometimes hopeful; sometimes heartbroken, sometimes whimsical; there is something between these pages for all your moods. From fresh experiences in the smoky coal-dust air of Dhanbad to the familiar air of the City of Nawaabs, explore a colourful bouquet of topics and relive the world through a poet's eyes. Enjoy.
