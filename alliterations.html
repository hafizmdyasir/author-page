Alliterations

January Journal - 2021

Alliterations acknowledges the beauty of love and family, the music of cheerful and witty verses, as well as the harsh sacrifices that hundreds around the world have made in the pursuit of unity and humanity. Its poignant imagery and profound metaphors are the perfect companion for the lovers of song and literature alike. Whether it's a doddering Sunday afternoon, or a sprightly Thursday morning, there is always a poem between these pages that will resonate with your mood.
